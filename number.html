How To Play


SnailNumber is a 24 hours long mini-game to let the community influence development.

Pick any number between 300 and 3000, and bid more than the previous maximum bid.
If the clock ticks to 0 without anyone else outbidding you, your number will be used in SnailTroi.
Not only that: you'll win part of the pot!

The pot is split between the winner and SnailThrone.
The share of this split starts at 100% for the winner and 0% for SnailThrone.
Then it decreases gradually, until 0% for the winner and 100% for SnailThrone.

IN SHORT:
The earlier you bid, the bigger rewards.
Be the leader when the clock hits 0, and you win.
The number you pick has no bearing on your chances of victory. It's purely a magic number for the NEXT game: SnailTroi.

Wonder what SnailThrone is?
SnailThrone is the master snail game, where holders can earn dividends from other snail games.
