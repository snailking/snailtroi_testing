How To Play


WHAT DO I NEED TO PLAY?


You will need a web3 compatible wallet, such as MetaMask for desktop, or Alpha Wallet for mobile.

You will also need ether (ETH), the currency of the Ethereum network.
ETH is used to pay for transaction fees, as well as play the game itself.

1/8

Grow your Troi with ETH to produce Froot.

When you Harvest Froot, you earn ETH.

Your rewards start at a minimum rate of 1% daily.
In addition, there is a global bonus growing up to 8% per day.

Your Froot is ready to Harvest every 24 hours.
Time your Harvest well to get the highest bonus!

2/8

Own any of the Four Squirrel Kings for special perks.

- BLUE KING: 4% in divs whenever any player Grows their Troi
- RED KING: doubles the effect you get from global bonus
- GREEN KING: 4% in divs whenever any player Harvests their Froot
- PURPLE KING: 4% of the pot when the Doomclock stops

Every King is unique, owned by a single player. When another player buys a King, the previous owner receives his ETH back + bonus.

Kings start at 0.02 ETH, and raise in price by 0.02 ETH with every flip. A player can own several Kings.

3/8

DAILY REWARD


Spend the most ETH in one Growth to earn 2% of the total pot at the end of the day!

Only one winner can earn the Daily Reward.

4/8

DOOMCLOCK


If the Doomclock ticks down to 0, Trois produce 10% less Froot.

To reset the Doomclock, Grow your Troi.
Minimum cost for a reset starts at 0.001 ETH, and rises by 0.001 ETH per Growth.

The last player who reset the Doomclock wins 4% of the pot when the Doomclock stops!

5/8

SNAILTHRONE


SnailTroi is part of the SnailThrone ecosystem.

SnailThrone is a master game where players earn dividends from all other snail games.
10% of the ETH invested in SnailTroi is saved in the thronePot as rewards to holders.

The thronePot is sent at the same time the Daily Reward is paid out, if there's at least 0.01 ETH in the thronePot.

Check out SnailThrone here: SNAILTHRONE

6/8

REFERRALS


On Froot harvesting, referrals give an extra 20% ETH to their referrer.
This is bonus ETH, not taken out of the referee rewards. There's no drawback to using referrals!

SnailTroi is best played with friends. Refer your buddies, have them refer you, it's all extra ETH in your pocket.

You are never locked into a referral. If you're unhappy with your referral, change it by Growing your Troi again.

For your referral link to be active, you need to own at least 420 snails in SnailThrone.
