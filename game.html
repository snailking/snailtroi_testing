How To Play


Grow your Troi with ETH to produce Froot. When you Harvest Froot, you earn ETH.

Your rewards start at a minimum rate of 1% daily. In addition, there is a global bonus growing up to 8% per day.
Harvest your Froot at the right time for the best rewards!

Own any of the Four Squirrel Kings to get special advantages:
- BLUE KING: 4% in divs whenever any player Grows their Troi
- RED KING: doubles the effect you get from global bonus
- GREEN KING: 4% in divs whenever any player Harvests their Froot
- PURPLE KING: 4% of the pot when the Doomclock stops

Every King is unique, owned by a single player.
When another player buys a King, the previous owner receives his ETH back + bonus.
Kings start at 0.02 ETH, and raise in price by 0.02 ETH with every flip.
A player can own several Kings.

DAILY REWARD: spend the most ETH in one Growth to earn 2% of the total pot at the end of the day!

DOOMCLOCK: if the Doomclock ticks down to 0, Trois produce 10% less Froot.
To reset the Doomclock, Grow your Troi. Minimum starts at 0.001 ETH, and rises by 0.001 ETH per Growth.
Whoever spent enough to reset the Doomclock last wins 4% of the pot when the Doomclock stops!

SNAILTHRONE: SnailTroi is part of the SnailThrone ecosystem.
10% of the ETH invested in SnailTroi is saved in the thronePot as rewards to holders.
If there is at least 0.01 ETH in the pot, the thronePot is sent at the same time the Daily Reward is paid out.
